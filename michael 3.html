Why ICT?

Why ICT? (5 Key Points) 1. Improves Communication: Enables fast and efficient global communication. 2. Enhances Education: Provides tools for e-learning and access to knowledge. 3. Boosts Economy: Drives innovation, creates jobs, and increases productivity. 4. Transforms Healthcare: Supports telemedicine and efficient health data management. 5. Simplifies Governance: Streamlines government services and increases transparency.

Why Gas?

Why Gas Strand? (5 Key Reasons) 1. Versatility: Gas-related skills are used in various industries like energy, manufacturing, and transportation. 2. High Demand: The gas industry requires skilled workers for exploration, production, and distribution. 3. Energy Sector Growth: Gas is a vital energy source, making expertise in this field essential. 4. Environmental Impact: Knowledge of gas systems helps in transitioning to cleaner energy solutions. 5. Global Opportunities: Careers in gas open doors to international job markets.

Why Stem?

Why STEM Strand? (5 Key Reasons) 1. Foundation for Innovation: STEM develops skills in science, technology, engineering, and math, driving technological advancements. 2. In-Demand Careers: Prepares students for high-paying, in-demand jobs in industries like healthcare, IT, and engineering. 3. Problem-Solving Skills: Enhances critical thinking and creativity to tackle real-world challenges. 4. Global Relevance: Provides opportunities to contribute to global progress in science and technology. 5. Versatile Education: Offers a strong foundation for further studies in specialized and interdisciplinary fields.
